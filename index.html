About Us

A clinic built on
expertise and trust.

The philosophy

Care that puts
you at the centre.

At Smile Bengaluru, every patient is seen as a whole person. Our goal is to make you feel calm, informed, and confident at every step, whether you're in for a cleaning or a complex procedure.

We follow a conservative care philosophy. If a simpler option exists, we choose it and explain why. Informed consent is not a formality here, it's the foundation of everything we do.

Evidence-based decisions. Honest communication. Long-term thinking. That's the standard we hold ourselves to.

"Good dentistry begins with listening. When a patient feels truly heard, everything else follows naturally."
Dr. Prithvi S. Balepur, Founder
Clinical ethics

Treatment is recommended only when clinically necessary. All options are explained clearly. Patient understanding and consent come before anything else.

Beyond the clinic

The surgeon
who plays sitar.

There is a particular patience that raga improvisation demands, a willingness to sit with complexity, to listen before acting, to trust that something beautiful emerges when you don't rush.

Dr. Prithvi has been a practicing Hindustani classical sitarist for decades, trained in the Maihar gharana tradition, the lineage of Pandit Ravi Shankar. He performs at concerts, cultural festivals, and hospital celebrations across Bengaluru.

He doesn't separate his music from his medicine. The same attentiveness. The same patience. The same belief that how you do something matters as much as what you do.

Musical lineage
Trained in the Maihar gharana, the tradition of Pandit Ravi Shankar, through his association with Pandit N.R. Rama Rao, Karnataka's legendary sitarist.
